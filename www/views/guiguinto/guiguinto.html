Guiguinto

Guiguinto began as a barrio of Bulakan, the former provincial capital of Bulacan.

History

Their Filipino guides would cry out, "Hinto" (stop). The Spaniards thought this was the name "Hihinto". The Spaniards substituted "Gui" (with hard "g") for the Tagalog "Hi".
